OASIS+ has six separate “families” of multi-agency contract vehicles, collectively referred to as OASIS+. The contracts and their requirements are as follows:

<CollectionList>
    <CollectionListItem  img_src="images/sba-icons/total-small-business.svg" img_alt="Total Small Business (SB) icon">
        ## Total Small Business (SB)
        All small businesses, including those designated by socioeconomic groups

        [Learn more about the Total Small Business (SB) program](https://www.sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-assistance-programs) 
    </CollectionListItem>

    <CollectionListItem  img_src="images/sba-icons/8-a.svg" img_alt="8(a) certified icon">
        ## SBA-Certified 8(a) Small Business (8a)
        SBA Offer & Approval only required for sole-source, direct award task orders

        [Learn more about the 8(a) Business Development program](https://www.sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-assistance-programs/8a-business-development-program) 
    </CollectionListItem>

    <CollectionListItem  img_src="images/sba-icons/hub-zone.svg" img_alt="HUB zone cerfified icon">
        ## SBA-Certified HUBZone Small Business (HZ)
        - Only certain businesses that are historically underutilized
        - Certified by SBA

        [Learn more about the HUBZone program](https://www.sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-assistance-programs/hubzone-program) 
    </CollectionListItem>

    <CollectionListItem  img_src="images/sba-icons/service-disabled-veteran-owned.svg" img_alt="Service-owned veteran-owned certified icon">
        ## SBA-Certified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB)
        - At least 51% owned and operated by veterans
        - SBA-certified at time of award

        [Learn more about the service-disabled veteran-owned program](https://www.sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-assistance-programs/veteran-contracting-assistance-programs) 
    </CollectionListItem>

    <CollectionListItem  img_src="images/sba-icons/wosb-certified.svg" img_alt="SBA Woman-owned small business certified icon">
        ## SBA-Certified Women-Owned Small Business (WOSB)
        - At least 51% owned and operated by women
        - SBA-certified at time of award

        [Learn more about the Women-Owned Small Business program](https://www.sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-assistance-programs/women-owned-small-business-federal-contract-program) 
    </CollectionListItem>

    <CollectionListItem  img_src="images/sba-icons/unrestricted.svg" img_alt="Unrestricted icon">
        ## Unrestricted (U)
        - Full and open; no restrictions
    </CollectionListItem>
</CollectionList>

## Contract awards
The OASIS+ UR contracts were awarded as full and open competition. The OASIS+ SB contracts were awarded as 100% small business set-asides. The OASIS+ 8(a) program was awarded as a 100% set aside for exclusive competition among 8(a) participants, which was offered to and accepted by Small Business Administration (SBA) under SBA requirement number IX1685025756D.
