OASIS+ will use the [Symphony](https://oasis.app.cloud.gov/) as the OASIS+ submission portal (OSP), for proposal submission, evaluations, and contract management. OASIS+ will use [GSA eBuy](https://www.ebuy.gsa.gov/ebuy/) for task order solicitations.

All sellers who are interested in submitting proposals to the OASIS+ suite of contracts must register in Symphony. In order to register your business in Symphony you will need your company SAM.gov Unique Entity ID (UEI).
