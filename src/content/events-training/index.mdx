We host events and offer a variety of training to help you learn how to use our contracts and buy professional services more efficiently and effectively. Training options include web-based, self-teaching tools, recorded webinars, podcasts, and on-site instructor-led.

##  Upcoming events 
- DPA training is scheduled to be released in May 2024. Links will be posted as registration becomes available. 

[Visit the OASIS+ Interact Community for updates and announcements.] (https://buy.gsa.gov/interact/community/196/activity-feed)

<SummaryBox header="Need to request a training?">
    The federal acquisition workforce and PSHC industry partners can request professional services related training. 
    - Web-based training: [Request training through the PSCH Training form](https://feedback.gsa.gov/jfe/form/SV_7OEVru7bhQgGp7M).
    - In-person training: Requires a minimum of 25 people and can be requested by emailing [oasisplusdpa@gsa.gov](mailto:oasisplusdpa@gsa.gov). When arranging training for a group, please indicate how many people  plan to attend in the email.
</SummaryBox>
